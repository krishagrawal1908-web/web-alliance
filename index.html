About WEB

Who We Are

WEB is a Kingshot alliance in Kingdom 813. We're a group of regular players who enjoy the game without taking it too seriously. No elitism, no gatekeeping — just a solid group of Governors who show up, help each other out, and have a good time doing it.


We've been around long enough to know what works: active members, decent coordination during events, and a chat that doesn't feel like a military briefing. If that sounds like your kind of alliance, you'll fit right in.

Our Mission

We're not here to stress you out. WEB is built on three simple pillars — grow steadily, stay chill, and be happy.


We believe the best alliances aren't the ones screaming orders 24/7 — they're the ones where Governors actually enjoy logging in. We grow at a pace that works for everyone: no burnout, no pressure, no drama. Whether you're a hardcore daily player or someone squeezing in 20 minutes before bed, you belong here.


We fight when it counts, donate when we can, and always look out for each other. The game should be fun — and in WEB, it is. Good vibes only. Kingdom 813 is our home, and we intend to keep it peaceful, powerful, and ours.

Alliance Rules

01

Stay Active

Log in, participate in events, and stay engaged. We're not going to chase you — but if you go quiet for too long, your spot may be given to someone who needs it.

02

Be Respectful

Treat everyone the way you'd want to be treated. No drama, no trash talk towards alliance members. We're all here to have a good time.

03

Play Free

Spender or free-to-play — it doesn't matter here. Everyone is welcome regardless of power or wallet size. We grow together at our own pace.

04

Keep It Chill

No pressure, no stress. This is a game — it should be fun. We don't bark orders or make you feel bad for missing an event. Relax and enjoy it.

05

Suggestions Are Always Welcome

Got an idea to improve the alliance, the events, or anything else? Say it. We're open to feedback from every governor — your voice matters here.
